TIPS Outcome Risk Analyzer

Browser-based tool built around the workflow used in pre-TIPS diastolic dysfunction studies. Upload a CSV, classify diastolic dysfunction, compare outcomes, run exploratory logistic regression, and export the analyzed dataset.

Clinical basis

This tool uses the core echo markers often applied in cirrhotic cardiomyopathy and post-TIPS outcome work: septal e′, E/e′, left atrial volume index, and tricuspid regurgitation velocity. The default rule classifies diastolic dysfunction when at least 3 of the 4 criteria are abnormal.
